Module 1

Overview

PSI in Checkpoint replaces the legacy ePSI tool by embedding post‑sale and postcard inspections directly into the Checkpoint application. The goal is to give technicians a single, mobile‑first workflow that standardizes inspections, improves arbitration support, and reduces operational friction.

🔄 What's Changing

  • Post‑Sale and Postcard Inspections are moving out of the legacy ePSI system and into Checkpoint, using the same mobile-first application already used for Certification inspections
  • Technicians will no longer need separate devices or workflows for CERT vs. PSI; both inspection types are completed in a single app
  • Consistent output available for every completed inspection
  • Improved handling of second check/soft fail scenarios

🎯 Program Goals

  • Retire the legacy ePSI laptop-based workflow and reduce dependency on specialized soft/hardware
  • Standardize inspection workflows and data capture across certification and post-sale inspections using shared components
  • Reduce inspection cycle time and operational cost through a unified, mobile-first experience
  • Improve arbitration support with complete, digital inspection data
  • Reduce operational friction by retiring ePSI laptops

🔧 Technicians

  • One device, one app for both CERT and PSI inspections — no switching between mobile devices and laptops
  • Guided, consistent inspection workflows that mirror certification inspections, reducing training overhead and inspection errors
  • Faster inspections with fewer handoffs and less duplicate data entry

📊 Non-Technicians (Coordinators, Admins, Arbitration)

  • Cleaner, more consistent PSI data delivered automatically into downstream systems, reducing rework and follow-ups
  • Improved visibility and confidence in inspection outcomes, since PSI data follows the same structured standards as CERT inspections
  • Standardized inspections and faster result delivery reduce arbitration disputes and operational risk.

Module 2

Key Changes in BETA

Four major changes you need to know about.

📱 Mobile-First Inspection

  • Technicians will use the PSI workflow in the Checkpoint app to perform post-sale and postcard inspections
  • No more switching between laptop-based ePSI and mobile devices — everything happens in one unified platform
  • One device in hand with seamless CERT-to-PSI transitions and faster workflows

⚙️ New Inspection Outcomes

  • Introducing new POST VCF status, VP (Problem with Move), which will represent any 2nd check activities (i.e. soft fail)
  • Allows teams to pause the inspection outcome and complete any necessary follow-up
  • Enables more informed final determinations with flexible decision-making and time for due diligence

📄 Automated Access & Documentation

  • A Mechanical Inspection Report is automatically generated after submission — regardless of Pass, Fail, or VP status
  • Post to AS400 F5 notes includes the PSI Results link and Tech Notes for easy access and review
  • Instant reports that are always accessible with complete transparency

🤝 Team Member Responsibilities

  • Managers play a critical role in ensuring smooth adoption and effective use of the new VP workflow
  • Educate teams on when and how to use VP vs. Pass or Fail
  • Establish clear ownership for who manages VP follow-ups at your site
  • Monitor execution and provide feedback on gaps or issues during beta

Goals & Outcomes

PSI Beta will be considered successful if teams consistently use Checkpoint for PSIs, inspections are completed faster with higher quality data, VP cases resolve quickly with clear ownership, and fewer issues escalated unnecessarily to arbitration.

⚠️ High Fail rate → VP criteria may be too loose or follow ups are adding little value

  • Consistent understanding of Pass vs. VP vs. Fail
  • Clear ownership of VP follow ups across teams

Module 13

2nd Check — Soft Fail

Inspection didn't clearly pass but hasn't officially failed either. Local auction team members handle the review, seller communication, or additional checks before making a decision.

✨ Key Benefits

🛡️

Prevent Premature Hard Fails

Avoid automatic arbitration when issues can be resolved through conversation and review.

💬

Allow Time for Seller Negotiation

Provides opportunity for clarification, seller discussions, and estimate review before final decision.

⚙️ Working with Soft Fails

1

Log In to Checkpoint

Access checkpoint.manheim.com to view and process 2nd Check inspections

2

Update Inspection Status

After review and resolution, update the inspection status to PASS or FAIL and add tech notes documenting the outcomes
